Frequently Asked question
Q. Can I learn all 64 skills?

While it's admirable to aspire to master all 64 Skills, each Kala is profoundly deep, and mastering even a handful could take a lifetime. At TAKE Global, our aim is to provide a platform for you to experience and explore the Kalas of your choice using our events an community sessions, allowing a personalized learning journey.


Q. Some skills seem irrelevant. Why should I adapt?

While it's true that not all 64 Skills may seem directly relevant, the diversity of the skills is intentional. Even if some may not align with your immediate goals, many offer therapeutic, emotional, mental, personal, professional, and social benefits. Adapting these skills can lead to holistic growth and well-being.


Q. How can 64 Skills be applied in modern life?

Personally, it shapes character; professionally, it builds leadership; socially, it fosters community, creating a comprehensive life strategy.
